Paper Format

Manuscripts should be submitted in english, using PDF format with a minimum of 11 pages, and formatted using the camera-ready templates available: Here . It is important that previous related work be appropriately cited. All submissions will be refereed for quality, originality, and relevance by the Program Committee. Accepted papers will be considered for “Best Paper Award.”
